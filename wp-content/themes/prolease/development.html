Real Estate Lease Administration Software by ProLease

Manage all your properties on one powerful platform

ProLease real estate software provides a fully functional system with everything administrators and directors need to manage leased, owned, and subleased properties. As part of the larger ProLease platform, the Real Estate module integrates seamlessly with our Lease Accounting and Equipment modules, giving tenants one comprehensive solution for all their financing and accounting needs.
